About

This system recommends crops and fertilizers based on soil and environmental conditions.based on the results farmers can make accurate decissions abot plantation,irrigation and fertilizer usage .That analyzes soil conditions using soil moisture sensors,npk sensors,pH sensors.the collected data is processed through a microprocessor and cloud system ton provide real time results on a farmers mobile app or web pages. This helps in improving crop growth while reducing execessive use of fertilizers and pesticides,thereby protecting soil health.
